About

The
Story

Scouted in Moscow ten years ago, I haven't stopped moving since. My path has taken me from Paris with Cartier to Japan with Uniqlo, and through Korea with Fila. Along the way I picked up the languages too: I now speak Russian, English, French and Korean. Recently I've been pursuing acting, landing my first role in a Korean film in 2026. Off set, you'll find me at CrossFit, Pilates or halfway up a rock climbing gym, because movement in all its forms is simply who I am. Nice to meet you!
